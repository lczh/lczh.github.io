Short Bio

I'm a second year PhD student at MIT Mathematics and I'm very fortunate to be advised by Professor Jon Kelner. I obtained my Bachelor and Master in Computer Science in Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Professor Gary Miller. I'm broadly interested in theoretical computer science and machine learning, especially the design of efficient algorithms. I'm particularly interested in optimization, randomized linear algebra and spectral graph theory. I'm also interested in speeding up the training process for large-scale machine learning systems.
